About me

I'm a Data Scientist based in London, UK and I'm currently working in Financial Services.

My job is to turn data into insight in engaging, accessible and innovate ways. I believe that a chain is only as strong as it's weakest link, so I embed good design through the entire data lifecycle. My expertise lies in extracting simplicity from complexity - which means building robust ETL pipelines, training efficient models, to preparing thoughtful, articulate and well-designed written, spoken and visual communications.
